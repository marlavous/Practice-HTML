How to make a sandwich

  1. Get bread, peanut butter, jelly and a knife
  2. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread
  3. Spread jelly on the other slice of bread
  4. Place slices of bread together with the peanut butter and jelly facing eachother
  5. Slice on a diagonal
